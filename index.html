The VHSS Advantage

Inspired by the teachings of Swami Vivekananda — we nurture not just students, but future leaders of Odisha.

Residential Campus

Safe, structured residential facility with separate hostels, nutritious meals, and 24/7 supervision for students from across the district.

📚

Expert Faculty

Highly qualified and experienced teachers dedicated to academic excellence. Regular doubt sessions, extra classes and personal mentorship.

🏆

Outstanding Results

Consistent 97%+ pass rate in CHSE Odisha. Multiple district toppers and state rank holders every year across all streams.

Modern Labs

Well-equipped Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Computer labs. Practical learning that goes beyond textbooks.

Competitive Exam Prep

Special coaching for JEE, NEET, CLAT and OAS integrated with regular curriculum. Students make it to top institutions nationwide.

Sports & Culture

Annual sports meet, cultural festivals, debates and NSS activities. Holistic development in the spirit of Swami Vivekananda.

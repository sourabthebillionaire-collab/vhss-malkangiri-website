Campus Life

Life at VHSS goes beyond classrooms — sports, culture, residential life and lifelong friendships.

Infrastructure

Our Facilities

🏠

Residential Hostel

Safe, comfortable hostel with separate wings for boys and girls. Nutritious meals, 24/7 security and a home-like environment.

🔬

Science Labs

Fully equipped Physics, Chemistry and Biology laboratories for hands-on practical learning and experiment-based education.

💻

Computer Lab

Modern computer lab with internet connectivity. Students gain digital literacy alongside their core subjects.

📖

Library

Well-stocked library with NCERT, reference books, magazines, newspapers and competitive exam materials.

⚽

Sports Ground

Spacious sports ground for cricket, football, volleyball and athletics. Annual sports meet celebrated with enthusiasm.

🍽️

Canteen & Mess

Hygienic canteen serving nutritious meals. Hostel mess provides balanced diet to residential students daily.

Grand sports meet with track events, team sports and prize distribution. Encourages physical fitness and healthy competition.

Annual reunion of VHSS alumni. Successful graduates inspire current students and share career guidance from their experiences.

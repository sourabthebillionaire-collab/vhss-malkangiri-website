Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence in Malkangiri

Vivekananda Higher Secondary +2 Junior Science College was established with a singular vision — to provide quality higher secondary education to the students of Malkangiri district, one of the most underserved regions of Odisha.

Named after the great philosopher-monk Swami Vivekananda, the college embodies his teachings of strength, service and self-reliance. We believe that education is not just about marks — it is about building character, confidence and a hunger for knowledge.

Today, VHSS stands as a beacon of hope for thousands of families in Malkangiri, Koraput and surrounding areas, consistently delivering results that rival the best colleges in Odisha.

🎯 Our Vision

To be the premier institution of higher secondary education in southern Odisha, producing students of academic brilliance and strong moral character.

❤️ Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, quality residential education inspired by Swami Vivekananda's ideals — nurturing every student's potential.

💡 Our Values

Integrity, academic rigor, discipline, compassion and service to society — the cornerstones of every VHSS graduate's character.

🏆 Our Commitment

Every student who walks through our gates gets personal attention, mentorship and the best preparation for their future career and higher studies.
